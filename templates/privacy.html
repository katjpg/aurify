Privacy Policy

✦ Information Collection and Use

Your privacy is our priority. We access your public Spotify profile and top tracks data to create unique, personalized visualizations. This data is processed in real-time and not stored, ensuring your privacy is upheld. Our goal is to provide an enhanced experience by responsibly handling your information.

✦ Revoking Permissions

To revoke Aurify's access to your Spotify data, simply visit your Spotify account's apps page. Locate Aurify and click "REMOVE ACCESS." This action will immediately cease all access we have to your Spotify data. For any assistance or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.
